# Video Generation Guide

This guide provides detailed instructions for integrating video generation tools such as OpenSora 2, Vivideo, and CapCut into your workflow for creating YouTube Shorts.

## OpenSora 2
1. **Installation**: Download OpenSora 2 from the official website and install it on your system.
2. **Setup**: Launch the application and configure your settings according to your needs.
3. **Creating Videos**: Select your desired video format and upload your media files. Use the built-in features to enhance your video.
4. **Exporting**: Once you are satisfied with your video, export it in the desired format suitable for YouTube Shorts.

## Vivideo
1. **Installation**: Visit the Vivideo website and download the latest version.
2. **Setting Up**: Open the program and complete the initial setup wizard.
3. **Video Creation**: Import your video clips, and utilize the editing tools to create engaging content.
4. **Export Options**: Choose the export settings tailored for YouTube Shorts.

## CapCut
1. **Access CapCut**: Download and install CapCut from the app store on your device.
2. **Create New Project**: Start a new project and add clips and music to your timeline.
3. **Editing Features**: Make use of various editing options like filters, texts, and effects to make your video stand out.
4. **Exporting**: Save your project in the format compatible with YouTube Shorts.

## Additional Tips
- Always keep your video duration under 60 seconds for YouTube Shorts.
- Use engaging thumbnails and titles to capture audience attention.
- Consider adding subtitles to your videos for better accessibility.
